Archigrad is a high-tech, open-source operation dedicated to making architecture informed.

What We Do

We specialize in real-time architecture computing, analyzing and visualizing concealed data to identify underperforming areas in infrastructure.

Our Strengths

  • Real-time data (30fps)
  • Autonomous operation (up to 2 months)
  • End-to-end problem solving
  • Multi-scale applications

Our Tech Stack

Custom hardware (esp32) and software solutions using JavaScript, PHP, Rust, Python, Houdini, and more.
